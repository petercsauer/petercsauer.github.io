i2robotics

i2robotics was a FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team that I started in my basement my freshman year of high school. After a rough start our first year, we managed to make it to the World Championships 3 years in a row, and got second in the World my senior year of high school.

Unfortunately, the team disbanded in 2017 so the website has been deleted. The YouTube page, however, is still active so feel free to check out any of our old robot reveal videos and other content.
